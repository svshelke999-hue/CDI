PROCEDURE IN DETAIL:
The patient was positioned supine on the traction table. Fluoroscopy confirmed access. Diagnostic arthroscopy revealed labral tearing from 1 to 3 o'clock with acetabular chondrosis. Labrum was mobilized, debrided to stable tissue, and secured with suture anchors to restore the seal. Femoral head-neck junction was contoured using burr to address cam lesion. Capsular closure performed with simple sutures.
